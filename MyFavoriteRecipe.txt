Classic Tomato Spaghetti

Ingredients:
- 400g spaghetti
- 1 clove of garlic
- 400g ripe tomatoes
- Olive oil
- Fresh basil
- Salt & pepper

Instructions:
1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of boiling salted water according to package instructions.
2. Peel and finely slice the garlic. Roughly chop the tomatoes.
3. Heat 3 tablespoons of olive oil in a large pan, add the garlic, and cook until golden.
4. Add the chopped tomatoes, a pinch of salt and pepper, and a few basil leaves.
5. Drain the spaghetti and toss it well with the tomato sauce.
6. Serve with fresh basil and a drizzle of olive oil.
